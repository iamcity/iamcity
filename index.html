You, Love and the City

IamCity is a platform that connects people to give attention to those little things that DO make a big impact.


Have you ever walked down the street, somewhere in your city, looked around and thought: Yaiks, this is so grose. Who will clean this up? Shall I do it? Nahh... you haven't done it.

Ever had little ideas that you think can make a huge impact, but you haven't done anything with it because it seems impossible to do it on your own?

And what if you are not alone? What if you can reach a whole bunch of people with the same thoughts, with just a few clicks?


Our goal is to help empower people and motivate them to just do it. It's actually very simple.

With an open source system, everybody can participate in city developments so that the city becomes yours.


Think, inspire en just do it because you ARE the city.
